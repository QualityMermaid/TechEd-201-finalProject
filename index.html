Bedtime story prompts made easy

This site can help you with thinking up a bedtime story for your little ones with the added freedom of having your own characters!

The Story Creation page will ask for your input to help create a custom story prompt. Things that can be added here is Main Characters Name, location story will take place

In the Stories page you will be able to view the created promts for your story.

Example

Once upon a time there was a "cat" called "Bugsy" who lived in a "tree".

Describe where "Bugsy" lives

One day they took a trip to the "garden" where they met a friend called "Meg".

Describe the "garden"

"Bugsy" and "Meg" spent the day playing "hide and seek" then went home and ate "fish".
